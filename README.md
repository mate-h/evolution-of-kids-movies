## Evolution of Kids Movies

This is a research paper on the Evolution of Kids Movies.

### Hypothesis

The first pixar movies primarily focus on individual growth and personal relationships, which are portrayed by more traditional character behavior. Their sequels that were released at least a decade later place greater emphasis on community and social responsibility portraying morally ambiguous characters.

Quantifiable measurements on the subtitle text:
- Usage of "I" instead of "we"
- Number of times characters, places are mentioned
- Which character has the most dialogue
- Emotional content / sentiment analysis, sentiment classification
- How much dialogue does a certain character have?
